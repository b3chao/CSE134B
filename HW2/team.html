Our Project: A Food Tracking Application

Our applicaiton will allow you to search from a database of food items in order to create lists of your favorite foods, grocery shopping lists, and weekly meal plans.

Users will be able to track the macronutrients of each meal or a combination of meals. Grocery shopping lists are also customized to each meal plan so that the user will know exactly how much ingredients are needed.

Using the Nutritionix API, our application can provide an extensive selection of meals to choose from to create innovative and better meal plans.
